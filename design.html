Web Design & Development Services for Small Businesses

Planning your new business website

Maybe your existing website needs a redesign to modernise and take advantage of some of the latest web technologies, or perhaps your business needs a completely new website from scratch. Whatever your website goals, we can help.

We will:
  1. Run an audit on your existing website if you have one
  2. Meet you in person to discuss your design preferences
  3. Gather your content and images to promote your business
  4. Build your new mobile-friendly, search engine optimised website
  5. Test your new website on a range of devices and browsers
  6. Launch your brand new website and promote it on your social networks

How much will a new website cost?

This will depend on your requirements but we charge a flat rate per project and require 50% upfront starting fee, with the final 50% payment on the day of your new site going live.

To get a free, no-obligation quote, just drop us an email with your requirements and we'll get back to you within 24 hours. We understand that small businesses have tight budgets and that's fine, we'll look at your requirements and prioritise the most important to ensure that your budget is spent for the best possible return.

All our basic websites will have the following features:

  • Responsive - will work on all the latest devices
  • Search engine optimised
  • Social Media feed integration
  • Modern design with call-to-actions to encourage sales

If you have specific requirements just ask, we love challenges and can work with almost any request if you have the available budget.

Want to update the code yourself?

A custom content management system will allow you to make regular updates to your website without paying a maintenance fee. Your website will be built on the content management system that is right for your website, this could be WordPress or it might be custom, but either way it will be simple, easy to use and will allow you to update all the content on your website. You will get some basic training on how to update your content if you need it. If you don't want to update your content regularly yourself, a static site may be the cheapest option but you will have to pay for a website care package if you want future updates.
